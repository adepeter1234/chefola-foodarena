ABOUT CHEF OLA

CEO Olabiyi Lawal is a serial entrepreneur and the CEO of Food Arena. He grew up in Nigeria’s most vibrant city, Lagos. His elementary influence was his mum who was into catering. His mum is a master of quick dinners whose uncanny ability to combine raw ingredients to create a flavourful meal is one he is glad to have inherited from her. His varied career after picking up some of his culinary skills from his mum included a stint at Kochtas Cousine as a food Service and grilling Caterer.

These peculiar experiences brought to his understanding the crucial need to have a broad and refined international knowledge which he was able to pursue further at Istanbul Gelism University, Turkey where he studied Gastronomy. After working at the prestigious Krunch Restaurant in Istanbul, he moved back to Nigeria. He then went ahead for a professional training at the Reddish Chronicles Culinary School, Lagos, Nigeria where he studied intercontinental and African cuisine from different professionals and graduated on top of the class.
